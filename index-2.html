Sean Palia
Rose, a former outlaw, travels through the spirit realm of the Underwest. Take up the once-forgotten skills of the gunslinger in this Action-Platformer adventure and journey along the breathtaking landscapes on a quest to rescue your daughter’s spirit.

About

1. Story

SeanPalia is a run and gun action platformer with style and story. With hand crafted scenes we deliver an ever shifting atmospheric experience. Full voice acting weaves a narrative throughout the action packed journey in the Underwest.

2. How it works?

Take control of your inner gunslinger with full 360 aiming and shooting allowing you to pick out troublesome targets from the horde of Underworld dwellers.

3. The concept

Move forward and survive each custom environment, stylised in comic and cinematic paneling. Capture the bounties of the deadly Sins of the Underworld in exchange for your daughter's freedom from her imprisonment within the locket.
